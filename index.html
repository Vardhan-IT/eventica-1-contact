Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my event?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for most events. For weddings and large corporate events, we suggest booking 6-12 months ahead to ensure availability of your preferred date and venue.

What is included in your event planning packages?

Our packages include venue selection, vendor coordination, event design, timeline management, and on-site coordination. Additional services can be customized based on your specific needs.

Do you offer virtual event planning services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive virtual event planning services, including platform selection, technical support, and engagement strategies to ensure your virtual event is successful.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies based on the type of event and services booked. We typically require a 30-day notice for cancellations, with some fees applicable. Please contact us for specific details.
